# Group Task – Contribution of Alan Turing to Artificial Intelligence

## Topic
Analyze and Present the Contribution of Alan Turing to AI

This group project explores Alan Turing’s foundational contributions to Artificial Intelligence, including the Turing Machine, the Turing Test, and his influence on modern intelligent systems.

### Key Areas Covered
- Turing Machine and Computation
- The Turing Test
- World War II Codebreaking
- Contribution to AI Development
- Legacy and Modern Impact
